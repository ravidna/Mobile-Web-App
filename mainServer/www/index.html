Order:

This page presents all products in the DB. You can easily choose quantity and add it to your cart.
You can even try to order large quantity and see what will happen (hint: we check that the quantity you want to buy is available in stock).
NOTE: products can only be added to cart. To remove products from cart - please use Checkout page.
Endpoints:
  • /api/products/ with GET: to get all products in Products table in DB.
  • /img/:productName/ with GET: to get a product image.
  • /api/stock/:productName/ with GET: to get the quantity left in stock, for the given product. Uses Products table in DB.
  • /api/order/addProduct/ with PUT: to add products to the cart (will not overwrite current cart). Your cart will be written to Orders table in DB.

On Sale!:

Presents only products which is on sale. You can easily choose quantity and add it to your cart.
You can even try to order large quantity and see what will happen (hint: we check that the quantity you want to buy is available in stock).
NOTE: products can only be added to cart. To remove products from cart - please use Checkout page.
Endpoints:
  • /api/sale/ with GET: to get all products on sale. Uses Products table in DB.
  • /img/:productName/ with GET.
  • /api/stock/:productName/ with GET.
  • /api/order/addProduct/ with PUT.

Package Deals:

Offers recommended packages of products to cook yourself a meal (breakfast, lunch and chef meal).
You can easily choose a package and its ingredients will be added to your cart.
The price of the package is being calculated by summing the ingredients' prices.
Endpoints:
  • /api/price/:productName/ with GET: to get the price of a given product. We use it for each ingredient in each package.
  • /api/order/addProduct/ with PUT.

Checkout:

Review your order, change quantities and even remove products from cart.
Finally, you can click on "Checkout" to place your order.
Endpoints:
  • /api/order/ with GET: to get user's current cart. Uses Orders table in DB.
  • /api/order/setOrder with PUT: to overwrite cart/order with a new order. Uses Orders table in DB.
  • /img/:productName/ with GET.
  • /api/price/:productName/ with GET.
  • /api/stock/:productName/ with GET.

Endpoints:

Register:

Register a new user.
Endpoints:
  • /users/register/ with POST: to register a new user. Verify given JSON and return a cookie for 30min. Use Users table in DB.
If you prefers Postman's GUI over ours, you can send a registration JSON to /users/register endpoint. Should be in the next format: {name: , user: , pass: }.
You must use a unique username! In case of successful registration you will get "0" in response, and a cookie üç™. Otherwise you gonna get "1".
